A Tribute to Alan Watts

Overview

Alan Watts was known for his witty views on religions, especially of Eastern descent. He had a very particular way of explaining "dodgy" topics that most would not delve into. He continually awed crowds with his jokes and light-heated nature.He had many quotes which were influential during the self-help movement as well as the spirituality movement which was re-occuring during his time.

Interesting Theories

Alan's way of thinking was one that has never quite been matched. He knew how to take any situation and make it understandable. Among his interesting theories, he continually stated that the world is opposites. He took the notion that everything is dependent on its opposites in order to exist. In other words, we cannot know evil unless we had good to compare it to. This is one of his most intersting theories. He also had a never ending list of stories about zen masters and way that they would awaken their students. His stories and way of speaking will live on for generations.
